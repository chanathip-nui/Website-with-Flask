App Play Refund Policy

At App Play, we want you to be satisfied with your digital purchases. Depending on the type of content you purchased, our refund policies may vary. Please review the guidelines below.

Apps & Games

  • Within 48 hours: You may be able to get a refund depending on the details of the purchase.
  • After 48 hours: We generally do not offer refunds, but you can contact the developer directly to troubleshoot issues.

Movies & TV Shows

  • If you haven't started watching the movie or show, you can request a refund within 7 days of your purchase.
  • If the content is defective, unavailable, or doesn't perform as stated, you can request a refund at any time.

Books

  • E-books: You can request a refund within 7 days of purchase, unless the book was part of a bundle or was rented.
  • Audiobooks: Refunds are only available if the audio file is defective or fails to play.

How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please visit your Order History. Find the specific order you would like to return, select it, and choose "Request a refund."

Note: Refund decisions are made at the sole discretion of App Play. Abuse of the refund policy may result in the denial of future refund requests.
