Frequently Asked Questions

How do I request a refund for an app?

You can usually request a refund within 48 hours of purchasing an app or game. Go to your Order History, find the item, and select "Request a refund".

How do I cancel a subscription?

To cancel a subscription, navigate to your Profile > Subscriptions. Select the subscription you want to cancel and tap "Cancel subscription".

Why isn't my app downloading?

Check your internet connection and ensure you have enough storage space on your device. If the problem persists, try clearing the cache for the App Play store.
